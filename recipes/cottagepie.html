Cottage Pie

Description

The homemade cottage pie will in fact be the greatest pie you've ever tasted. Note: no cottages were hurt during the making of this pie!

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince (200g)
  • Mr Mashy's Mashed Potato (1 packet)
  • Grated Cheese
  • Onion
  • Condiments such as paprika, turmeric, black pepper/li>

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven to pre-heat at 160 degrees, then wash your hands you filthy animal
  2. Mix the mince and onion and add condiments.
  3. Spread evenly into the bottom of an oven dish
  4. Add boiling water to the Potato mix and stir until fluffy
  5. Spread potato across the top of the mince mix
  6. Sprinkle plenty of cheese and don't be shy!
  7. Add a sprinkle of black pepper and paprika
  8. Place in over and cook for 35 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and serve - enjoy (but don't burn ya mouth!)
